The platform is already enterprise-grade and has a real high-assurance posture.
However, it still has survivability-oriented operational debt because:
- signer trust still depends too much on a local file-backed trust-store assumption,
- archive storage still depends too much on a filesystem-centric assumption,
- backup/restore/migration verification is not yet a first-class operating flow,
- trust-store and archive recovery posture is not yet strong enough for long-lived disaster-aware operations,
- and reviewability after infrastructure migration or rebuild can still be made more durable.
