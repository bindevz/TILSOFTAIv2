The platform is already enterprise-grade and has a real high-assurance posture.
However, it still has durability-boundary debt because:
- trust-store recovery is still too dependent on file-backed local storage assumptions,
- archive durability is still too dependent on filesystem-family storage assumptions,
- production-like policy is not yet explicit enough about backend durability classes,
- retention and immutability posture are not yet surfaced strongly enough as backend capabilities,
- and recovery is still more “available” than “auditable as a control”.
